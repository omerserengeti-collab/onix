Setup Guide for Mac

Follow these steps after downloading (first time only)

1
Open the DMG and drag to Applications
Double-click the downloaded file and drag the Onix icon into your Applications folder.
📦 ➡️ 📁

Drag Onix.app → Applications

2
Open Onix — you'll see a warning. That's OK!
Apple will say it can't verify the app. Click "Done" (not "Move to Trash"). Then go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and scroll down.
3
Click "Open Anyway"
You'll see "Onix was blocked to protect your Mac" with an "Open Anyway" button. Click it, enter your password, and you're done!

🎉 That's it!

Onix is now installed. This process is only needed once.
Next time, just double-click to open.
